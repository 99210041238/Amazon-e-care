About


Amazon e-Care is a healthcare app designed to support pregnant women and people with disabilities. It features a simple sign-up process requiring basic details like name, mobile number, address, and pincode. In emergencies, such as when a pregnant woman is alone and experiencing pain, a "Help" button sends alert signals to relatives, nearby hospitals, and ambulances, ensuring swift assistance. Utilizing AWS Cloud for data storage, Google Maps API for location services, and advanced alert technology, Amazon Care aims to provide reliable, critical support. While its success depends on widespread adoption and reliable internet access, the app's innovative approach holds strong potential for significant impact..
